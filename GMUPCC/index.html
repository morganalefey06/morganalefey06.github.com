Welcome to George Mason University's Online Plaster Cast Catalogue.You can browse and search our plaster cast collection and learn about both the casts themselves and the art they were created from. Originally obtained from the Metropolitan Museum of Art in 2003, the collection contains plaster casts created from the art of several periods including Ancient Greece and Modern Art.
