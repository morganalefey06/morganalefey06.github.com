On loan from The Metropolitan Museum of Art

When this piece was discovered in the Campus Martius (Field of Mars) in Rome, the relief was found with three other panels, which were decorated with a marine procession of Tritons and Nereids celebrating the marriage of Poseidon and Amphitrite. The panel depicts a Nereid on a sea-bull, bringing a present for the wedding of Poseidon and Amphitrite.
